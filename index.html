Clendon Biscette

Lecturer in Digital Media (Front End Web Development) at Sir Arthur Lewis Community College and an avid E-Learning enthusiast.

Although I had never envisioned myself as a Web Developer, I am now at a place in my professional life where I am training and nurturing future web developers and web coders. My BSc. In Information Systems & Management pushed me gently towards computer hardware and software and that is where my professional experience began. While doing that I started my own Graphic Design Business and I guess you can say that this is where the web design seed was planted.

From there I started my career in education and spent 12 years teaching ICT to young learners from the age of 13 to 17. During that time, I got my teaching certificate in Secondary Education and that solidified my status as an educator. I was still not a hard-core web developer yet, but I managed to commercially develop a few websites through my own business. This started me on a movement towards enhancing my skills in Web Development and before long I was spending hours honing those skills.

Since then, I have married the two strands of my professional life, Education and Web Development. I have gone from facilitating at web coding bootcamps to my current contract as a lecturer of Front-End Web Development and Mobile App Development.
